MS Power BI Sales Dashboard

The sales dashboard is a tool that provides a comprehensive view of your key performance indicators while also revealing the method your sales team utilizes in monitoring and visualizing your business goals and revenue targets.

The goal of this dashboard is to quickly understand the team's performance, manage the pipeline and keep track of sales targets. It provides transparency into the sales KPI, which enables the sales team to assess the performance against the goals and objectives in real time.

The sales dashboard is a visual representation of sales data. Total sales by customer, sub-category, and city are represented by a clustered bar chart to concisely compare the values from each category. A comparison of sales and the target is demonstrated by a gauge chart to see how far you are from reaching a goal or target. A pie chart helps to show the percentages of the quantity by segment. The total sales and profit are demonstrated by an area chart to plot change over time (years, months, and days) or categories and draw attention to the total value across a trend. The total sales, profit, quantity, total no. of orders, and total discount are displayed through a card for easy viewing. It should also contain the state, category, ship mode, and ship date for easy filtering of data.
